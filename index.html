About Me

Hi my name is Jeff and I am a web developer living in Jackson, Wy. I graduated from Elon University in 2016 with a Bachelors Degree in Business Management and a minor in Asian Studies, and recently completed The University of Denver's full stack coding bootcamp in June, 2021. I started coding on my own as a way to challenge myself and learn a new skill. The fusion of creativity and logic sparked my intest and I soon found myself pursuing a new carrer rather than hobby. When I am not coding, I can be found exploring Jackson's surrounding mountains and rivers. My favorite hobbies include skiing, mountain biking, rafting, surfing, scuba diving, rollerblading and fly fishing.
